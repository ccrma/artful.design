The Birth and Rise or the Laptop Orchestra

Read Excerpt from Artful Design

“That the notion of a ‘laptop orchestra’ is seemingly paradoxical is one of my prime motivations for creating one; the pairing of these two inventions is perhaps obvious only because of its apparent impossibility. One is an almost archaic institution whose continued existence is something of a miracle, the other a technological newcomer that has become commonplace and seems likely to be with us, at least in some form, for quite some time. One serves to perform primarily European music from centuries ago, while the other is a convenient tool for editing text, crunching numbers, browsing the Web, and checking e-mail. Never the twain shall meet.”
—Dan Trueman
